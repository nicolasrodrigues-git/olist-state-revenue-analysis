
# Revenue Concentration Analysis – Brazilian E-commerce (Olist Dataset)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes revenue distribution across Brazilian states using real e-commerce transaction data. The goal is to identify revenue concentration patterns, regional performance disparities, and business opportunities for market expansion.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Understanding where revenue is generated is critical for strategic decision-making. Many businesses operate without clear visibility into geographic performance, which can lead to missed opportunities in underperforming regions.

This analysis addresses:

- Which states generate the most revenue?
- How concentrated is revenue distribution?
- Where are the growth opportunities?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Data Aggregation & Analysis

---

## 📊 Key Results

- **Total revenue analyzed:** R$ 15,846,795.12
- **Top 5 states contribution:** 73.18% of total revenue
- **Highest revenue state:** São Paulo (SP) ~37%
- Strong revenue concentration in the Southeast region

---

## 📈 Business Insights

- Revenue is highly concentrated in a small number of states
- São Paulo dominates total revenue generation
- Significant long-tail distribution across lower-performing states
- Clear opportunities for expansion in underpenetrated regions

---

## 💡 Strategic Impact

This analysis can support:

- Regional marketing strategies
- Resource allocation decisions
- Expansion planning
- Sales prioritization by geography
